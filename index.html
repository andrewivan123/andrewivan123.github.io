Andrew Ivan Soegeng

PhD Student in Natural Language Processing

National University of Singapore

I am a PhD student at the National University of Singapore (NUS). My research centers on Multilingual Large Language Models and Cross-lingual Transfer — specifically, enabling models to leverage knowledge acquired in one language and apply it effectively across all other languages. I am particularly interested in vocabulary adaptation and cross-lingual alignment as mechanisms for closing the gap between high-resource and low-resource languages.
